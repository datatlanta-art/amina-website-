About

About HiAmina

HiAmina is a collection of practical tools designed to make complex things easier to understand. Each product focuses on a real-world problem — whether that is understanding a car deal, improving a resume, or exploring neighborhoods.

The goal is simple: take messy information and turn it into clear, useful insight. The AMINA platform combines behavioral science, structured thinking, and AI to create tools that are practical, calm, and easy to use.

Origin

How It Started

HiAmina started as a personal project. I kept running into situations where decisions were harder than they needed to be — not because the choices were difficult, but because the information was confusing.

I began building small tools to make things clearer for myself. Over time, those tools grew into a larger system. Today, AMINA is focused on building practical tools that help people understand complex situations and make better decisions.

Founder

Josh Carter

I studied Behavioral Neuroscience at the University of Georgia, focusing on how people think, make decisions, and interpret information. I later completed a Business Analytics certificate, also from UGA.

Before building tools, I spent time in insurance — two years in claims evaluation, learning how much damage an incomplete picture can do, and two years in sales, learning how to make complex things understandable to people who didn't want to think about them.

I've spent 20 years across multiple industries in sales and customer-facing roles. That experience — combined with a background in risk analysis and decision science — is what led me to build HiAmina.

I build everything myself. Full-stack development, AI architecture, product design. I use AI when it earns its place — and have pulled it out entirely when deterministic logic was more reliable. Strategy before automation.
